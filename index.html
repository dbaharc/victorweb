Our Story

More Than a Haircut.
It's a Tradition.

Victor's Hair Salon has been a cornerstone of the Shaw neighborhood for decades. Victor, a fourth-generation barber with over 30 years of experience, brings a rare combination of old-school mastery and modern technique to every cut.

Alongside his wife Irma — equally accomplished with 30 years of her own — they've built something uncommon: a true family salon where every client is treated like a regular, every cut is given full attention, and every person leaves feeling their absolute best.

Nestled on T Street NW in the heart of Shaw, it's the kind of place the neighborhood is proud to call its own. Walk-ins always welcome.
